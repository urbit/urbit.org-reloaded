The ideas of urbit, from a clean-slate OS and network to self-sovereign digital identity and overlay systems, have sparked the interests of people around the world. And many have published their related thoughts and ideas. If you find yourself curious or yearning for more insights into the Urbit project, we recommend you give them a read:

- [subject.network](https://subject.network/blog)
- [~sarlev's blog](https://sarlev-sarsen.rooftopdao.io/blog)
- [Martian Computing](https://martiancomputing.substack.com)
- [the %hawk blog](https://hawk.computer/~~/misc/)

Or if you are really yearning for content, check out the [Urmanac](https://www.urmanac.com/wiki/~/p/~bordex-ripdur/urmanac/), an almanac of all things Urbit, focused on the history of apps on urbit.
